## Filming Tips

### Equipment Needed
- **Phone**: Any modern smartphone (iPhone 12+, Android flagship)
- **Tripod**: For stable shots
- **Lighting**: Ring light or natural window light
- **Microphone**: Lavalier mic for voiceover (optional)

### Recording Settings
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Format**: MP4
- **Lighting**: Bright, even lighting
- **Background**: Clean, minimal
