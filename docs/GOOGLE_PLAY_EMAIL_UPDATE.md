# Updating Email in Google Play Console

## New Email Address
**reach.hosteze@gmail.com**

## Steps to Update Email in Google Play Console

### 1. Update Developer Account Email
1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console)
2. Sign in with your developer account
3. Click on **Settings** (gear icon) in the left sidebar
4. Select **Account details**
5. Update the **Contact email** to: `reach.hosteze@gmail.com`
6. Click **Save**

### 2. Update App Contact Information
1. In Google Play Console, select your app (HostEze/Gatherly)
2. Go to **Policy** → **App content**
3. Update the following sections:

#### Privacy Policy
- Click **Privacy policy**
- Verify the URL points to your privacy policy
- The privacy policy already has the new email: `reach.hosteze@gmail.com`
- Click **Save**

#### Data safety
- Click **Data safety**
- Review the contact information
- Update developer email if shown
- Click **Save**

#### App access
- Click **App access**
- Update contact email if requested
- Click **Save**

### 3. Update Store Listing
1. Go to **Store presence** → **Main store listing**
2. Scroll to **Contact details**
3. Update **Email** to: `reach.hosteze@gmail.com`
4. Update **Website** if needed
5. Click **Save**

### 4. Update Data Deletion Instructions (if applicable)
1. Go to **Policy** → **App content**
2. Click **Data deletion**
3. Verify the URL points to: `/delete-account.html`
4. The page already has the new email: `reach.hosteze@gmail.com`
5. Click **Save**

### 5. Update in App Information
1. Go to **Store presence** → **Store settings**
2. Update **Developer contact** to: `reach.hosteze@gmail.com`
3. Click **Save**
