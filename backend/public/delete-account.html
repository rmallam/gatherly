Delete Your Gatherly Account

We're sorry to see you go! This page explains how to delete your Gatherly account and all associated data.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All events you've created
  • All guest lists and RSVP data
  • Event details (venue, catering, tasks, etc.)
  • Any contacts you've saved in the app

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

You can delete your account directly from the Gatherly app:

  1. Open the Gatherly app
  2. Go to your Profile or Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Email Request

To request account deletion via email, send a message to:
