Privacy Policy for Gatherly

Last Updated: December 12, 2024

Introduction

Gatherly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)

Event and Guest Information

When you use the app, you may provide:

  • Event details (title, date, location, description)
  • Guest information (names, phone numbers, email addresses)
  • RSVP responses
  • Check-in status

Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning QR codes to check in guests at events. No photos or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • Contacts: Used only when you choose to import contacts to add as guests. Contact access is optional and only occurs when you explicitly select the import feature.
  • Biometric Authentication: Used for secure login with fingerprint or face recognition (optional).

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the event management service
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Store and organize your events and guest lists
  • Enable QR code check-in functionality
  • Send email verification and account notifications
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All user data is stored securely on our servers
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Communications between the app and server are encrypted using HTTPS
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data

Camera Usage

Important: The camera permission is used solely for scanning QR codes to check in guests at your events. We do not:

  • Capture or store any photos or videos
  • Record or transmit camera data
  • Use the camera for any purpose other than QR code scanning

The QR code scanner only processes visual data in real-time to detect and decode QR codes. No images are saved to your device or our servers.

Contact Access

Contact access is completely optional. When you choose to import contacts:

  • You are prompted to grant permission each time
  • Only the contacts you explicitly select are imported
  • Contact data is used solely to populate guest information
  • We do not access or store contacts without your explicit action

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • When you share event invitations with guests (only event details you choose to share)
  • If required by law or to comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Revoke permissions (camera, contacts) at any time through device settings
  • Export your event and guest data

Children's Privacy

Gatherly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
